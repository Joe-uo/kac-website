About Kind & Compassionate Care

A family-owned business dedicated to providing kind and compassionate care for vulnerable children and adults with additional needs.

Our Story

Kind & Compassionate Care is a family-owned business born from personal lived experience. We are passionate about providing care for vulnerable children and adults, with deep roots in the autism community through our own family. Our founders have navigated the challenges of accessing appropriate services and advocacy, and we've channeled that knowledge and expertise into creating a business dedicated to supporting others on similar journeys.

Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to kindness, compassion, and person-centered care. We understand the unique needs of our service users, and we've built our services around what truly matters: independence, dignity, and genuine support.

Properties

We own and manage several properties specifically designed to support children, adolescents and young adults in transitioning into independent living. Each property is equipped with the necessary support systems and staff.

Care Packages

We provide tailored care packages for individuals living independently. Our support workers and personal assistants are available to provide the help needed, whether at our properties or in clients' own homes.

Fully Regulated

All our services are CQC (Care Quality Commission) compliant, ensuring the highest standards of care, safety, and quality. We are committed to continuous improvement and best practices.

Our Core Values

Compassion

We lead with kindness and empathy in everything we do. Our team understands that behind every service request is a person with unique needs, aspirations, and challenges.

Community

We believe in building strong community connections. We partner with councils, charities, and employment services to provide holistic support to our clients.

Empowerment

We focus on building independence and confidence. Our goal is not just to provide care, but to help our clients achieve their goals and live life on their own terms.

Excellence

We are committed to the highest standards in care delivery. Our CQC compliance and continuous improvement processes ensure we're always providing the best service possible.
