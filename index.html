Our Mission: Kind & Compassionate Care

At Kind & Compassionate Care, we are a family-owned business passionate about providing personalized care for autistic and ADHD individuals and adults.
With lived experience and professional expertise, we're dedicated to supporting independence and well-being.
All our services are CQC compliant.

What We Offer

We provide comprehensive support services including supported living accommodation to help adolescents and young adults transition into independent living. Our care packages and services are tailored to individual needs, and we support our clients wherever they are living. Every aspect of our service is delivered with compassion and professional care.

Our Experience

Founded by a family with lived experience of autism, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities. Our team combines professional qualifications with personal insight.

Safe & Regulated

All our services are CQC compliant, ensuring the highest standards of care, safety, and quality. Safeguarding is at the heart of everything we do.

Holistic Support

From accommodation and daily living support to employment referrals and skill development, we provide comprehensive services tailored to each individual.

Why Choose Kind & Compassionate Care

Family-Owned & Values-Driven

We're not just a service provider—we're a family business dedicated to making a real difference in the lives of autistic and ADHD adults, driven by personal experience and genuine care.

Lived Experience Expertise

Our founders and team members have personal experience supporting autistic individuals. This means we truly understand the needs and aspirations of those we serve.

Comprehensive Properties

We own several properties designed to support the transition to independent living. Each property is equipped to provide the support and environment our residents need to thrive.

24/7 Dedicated Support

Whether in our accommodation or living independently, we're here with 24/7 support. We also work with councils, charities, and employment services to maximize opportunities.
