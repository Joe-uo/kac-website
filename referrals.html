Submit a Referral

We accept referrals from UK councils and private sources. Submit a referral to get started with our services.

How to Submit a Referral

We welcome referrals from a variety of sources and make the process as straightforward as possible. You can submit a referral using our online form below, or contact us directly for assistance.

Who Can Refer?

  • Local Councils: We accept referrals from councils across the UK for eligible individuals requiring support services.
  • Private Referrals: We also accept private referrals from families, individuals, and other organizations.
  • Healthcare Professionals: GPs, social workers, and other healthcare professionals can refer clients to our services.
  • Self-Referrals: Individuals seeking our services can refer themselves through our online form.

Our Process

  1. Submit your referral through our online form or contact us directly
  2. We will review your referral and contact you within 3-5 business days
  3. We'll discuss your needs and explore how our services can support you
  4. If suitable, we'll develop a personalized care plan
  5. Services begin once all agreements are finalised

Frequently Asked Questions

What information do I need to provide?

We'll need basic contact information, details about the individual seeking support, their specific needs, and the type of service you're interested in. The more information you can provide, the better we can assess how we can help.

How long does the process take?

We aim to respond to all referrals within 3-5 business days. Initial assessment and planning typically take 2-4 weeks, depending on complexity and service availability.

Do you work with councils?

Yes, we actively work with councils across the UK. We accept both council-funded referrals and self-funded arrangements. Our team can discuss funding options with you.

What if we have urgent needs?

For urgent situations, please contact us directly by phone. We will do our best to accommodate urgent referrals and discuss emergency support options with you.
