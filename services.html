Our Services

Supported Living & Accommodation

We provide supported living accommodation specifically designed to support the transition to independence for adults with additional needs, particularly autism, neurodevelopmental and mental health conditions. Our services include:

  • 24/7 on-site support in our properties
  • Person-centered care plans tailored to individual needs
  • Support with daily living activities and life skills development
  • Coordination with local councils and charities
  • Links to employment support services
  • Skill development programs
  • Community integration support

Our properties are designed to be safe, supportive environments that promote independence while providing the structure and assistance our residents need to thrive.

24/7 Support

Dedicated staff available round the clock to support our residents.

Home Care Services

For those who wish to live independently but need ongoing support, we offer flexible care services delivered in your own home or living space. Our services include:

  • Domiciliary care workers
  • Personal Assistants (PAs)
  • Support workers for daily living and community integration
  • Assistance with household management and budgeting
  • Support with social and community activities
  • Health and wellness support

Our care workers are trained, compassionate professionals. We work closely with you to understand your unique needs and provide support that enhances your independence rather than limiting it.

Flexible Support

Tailored care adapted to your specific needs and preferences.

Why Choose Our Services

CQC Compliant

All our services are regulated by the Care Quality Commission, ensuring consistent quality, safety, and best practice standards across all aspects of our care delivery.

Person-Centered

We believe in tailoring support to individual needs, preferences, and goals. Your aspirations and independence are at the heart of everything we do.

Coordinated Support

We work collaboratively with councils, charities, employment services, and other organizations to ensure our clients have access to comprehensive support networks.

Compassionate Team

Our staff are trained, experienced professionals who genuinely understand autism and ADHD. We lead with compassion in every interaction.

Areas of Support

We provide comprehensive assistance across multiple life domains:

Accommodation

Safe, supportive properties designed for independent living with appropriate levels of staff support.

Employment

Links to employment support services and referrals to help find meaningful work opportunities.

Skill Development

Programs focused on building independence and life skills including cooking, budgeting, and community engagement.

Community Integration

Support with social activities and community participation to build connections and combat isolation.

Daily Living Support

Assistance with household management, personal care, and health and wellness activities.

We liaise with councils, charities, and other services to ensure coordinated, holistic support.
